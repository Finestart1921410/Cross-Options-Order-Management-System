About Us

Established in 1979, Cross Options was an early market maker in open outcry trading at the European Options Exchange (EOE) in Amsterdam. As it grew and merged with exchanges like Euronext and NYSE, it expanded into diverse markets, including the German Eurex. Adapting to screen-based trading, Cross Options invested in advanced technology, developing specialized software for changing market conditions. Beyond trading, the company's group ventures span pharmaceutical research and development and various financial activities.
